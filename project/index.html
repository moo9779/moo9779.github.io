The goal of this educational website is for any history enthusiast and curious minds that want to know the most important and consequential battles in United States history.

The United States has been in numerous conflicts but these eleven battles have shaped American laws, policies, national strategies and/or cultures.

In order to look to our present and future, we have to look at our past. With these battles, I hope to give a glimpse of insight into where the United States came from and how to prevent the same mistakes from happening again in the future.
